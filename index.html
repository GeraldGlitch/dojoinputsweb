Execution training redefined.

DojoInputs is a lightweight tool to record, practice, and master combos for Brawlhalla and other PvP games.

Most players watch slow-motion videos and try to replicate them. DojoInputs lets you physically feel the rhythm through real-time feedback and step-by-step practice.

Built for real execution training, not just theory.

Core Capabilities

Everything you need to stop missing that one crucial input.

Record & Master

Record combos using keyboard or gamepad (Xbox and compatible controllers) with millisecond precision.

Visual Feedback

Practice combos step by step with real-time visual feedback. Know exactly where you dropped it.

Export/Import

Share your tech. Export and import combos using .dojofiles files instantly.

The Developer

Meet Gerald Glitch

I'm a developer and competitive player who believes that mastery comes from deliberate practice. I built DojoInputs because I was tired of guessing why I missed my combos.

DojoInputs is my way of giving back to the community that makes fighting games great. My goal is to provide the tools that help every player reach their full potential.
